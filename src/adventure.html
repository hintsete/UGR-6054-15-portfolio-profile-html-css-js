Old Crafts

Along with the vinyls, I came upon ancient crafts from Emperor Haile Selassie's reign. These items, which range from hand-carved silver sculptures to finely designed pottery, represent Ethiopia's artistry and skill at a time of rapid cultural development. In addition to the crafts, there were also history books, albums, and paintings that illustrated the cultural richness of the era. Each piece's craftsmanship was a monument to the abilities and ingenuity of the time. It was an unforgettable experience to handle these items that reflect the story of Ethiopia's rich creative and cultural legacy.
