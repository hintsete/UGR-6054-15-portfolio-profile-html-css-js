Listening to Jazz Music

My interest in music drew me to the rhythmic and deep depths of Ethio-jazz, a genre that combines traditional Ethiopian sounds with jazz components. Mulatu Astatke, whose revolutionary works have captivated listeners all around the world, is one of my biggest influences. I had the wonderful experience of attending one of his gigs in Addis Ababa at the African Jazz Village in Ghion Hotel, where I witnessed him play live, bringing his music to life in the most captivating way. It was a truly memorable night, filled with the magic of his artistry. I am glad to provide a sample of one of his works below, offering a glimpse into this vibrant and rich musical legacy.
